- Declines account setup for now.

Scene 5 – Anchorfall Inn
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

- Later joins Tavian in conversation.
- Tavian assesses Borian as honorable.

Scene 6 – Evening Rest
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Borian completes a meditative trance.
- Room inspected earlier; no threats present.
